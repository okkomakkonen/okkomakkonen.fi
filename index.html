About

I am a quantum algorithm researcher at QMill, a Finnish quantum algorithm startup. In 2025, I graduated with a doctoral degree in Mathematics from Aalto University, Finland, under the supervision of Professor Camilla Hollanti. My doctoral research includes applications of coding theory in information-theoretically secure distributed computing, especially the applications of algebraic geometry codes and coset codes. My master's thesis on the same topic was awarded the 2023 Ernst Lindelöf prize for the best master's thesis in mathematics by the Finnish Mathematical Society. For 2023—2025 I was supported by the Vilho, Yrjö and Kalle Väisälä Foundation of the Finnish Academy of Science and Letters.
